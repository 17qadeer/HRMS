Company Policies

  • Work Hours: Our standard work hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
  • Remote Work: We offer flexible remote work options for eligible employees. Please refer to the Remote Work Policy for details.
  • Leave Policy: Employees are entitled to 15 days of annual leave per calendar year. Please refer to the Leave Policy for details.
  • Dress Code: Our dress code is business casual. Please ensure that your attire is appropriate for the workplace.
  • Code of Conduct: We expect all employees to adhere to our Code of Conduct at all times. Please familiarize yourself with the company's values and guidelines.

For more information, please refer to the Employee Handbook.
